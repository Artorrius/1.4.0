Liverpool Apex University - Liverpool

Apex Liverpool university was, as the name suggests, a building project for the Liverpool university that we were contracted to carry out some unique work for. The building in question had been designed to bear an aesthetic resemblance to the ship, The Titanic.

Our task was to clad the flues, which were then attached on to the top of the building to resemble the funnels of a ship. This was accomplished with an ash and lacy, mesh system (see pictures below). due to the size of the flues, their unique shape and the access on the roof, we fitted the cladding on site in York and the flues were then transported to Liverpool and fitted on site. After the flues were complete 2 of our team went to Liverpool to put the finishing touches to them and oversee some cladding works to the building.
